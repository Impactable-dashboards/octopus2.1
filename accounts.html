How to read this room

Engagement and movement, by name

Channel- and keyword-level revenue lives in Channel and Summary (Dreamdata). What the DemandSense set adds is the layer that is most actionable for targeting: who is engaging, how intensely, and whether they are heating up or cooling down — comparing a 30-day window against the 90-day baseline. Account-level revenue is not broken out in this data, so this room leads with movement, which is the better signal for where the next dollar of outreach goes. Tiers: Touched = 1+ impression · Aware = 30+ impressions · Engaged = 3+ clicks. Post-pivot, these accounts are where fit practitioners cluster — the target is the person (Platform/DevOps/SRE) inside the account, routed to the free tier, not the logo itself.

The signal

Financial services is the story.

The biggest recent movers skew hard to banks and financial institutions — JPMorganChase, Capital One, Mastercard, Wells Fargo, Deutsche Bank, BNP Paribas, Bloomberg, Revolut, Bank of America are all building momentum. Financial services and banking are roughly 39% of the high-confidence fit set (47 of 122), and they map onto the one message no competitor owns — the audit moment.
