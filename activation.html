The FSI audit-moment track

Why it gets its own track

Proving deployment governance to an auditor.

The banks are the accounts warming now — JPMorganChase, Capital One, Mastercard, Wells Fargo, Deutsche Bank, BNP Paribas, Bloomberg, Revolut. They share one pain no competitor markets to. This track leads with the hook "could you prove who deployed what, where, and when before an auditor asks?", targets the compliance persona alongside the budget owner, and layers ABM ad retargeting as air cover on the same accounts the client's July compliance search campaigns are chasing.
