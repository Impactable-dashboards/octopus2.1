How to read LinkedIn. LinkedIn is measured at the top of the funnel, so it will always show a lower direct return than branded search — its job is to make target accounts aware of Octopus before they ever search. Roughly a third of its influenced pipeline sits below LinkedIn's privacy reporting thresholds, so its true contribution is understated. Judge the two together, not in isolation.

The proof point

Your highest-returning keyword is your own brand name.

$4.6K on "octopus deploy" returned $472K in pipeline and $330K in closed-won. Branded search does not grow on its own — it grows when more of the right people know Octopus exists. That is what LinkedIn reach buys. Cutting the top of the funnel to chase a higher in-channel LinkedIn return would starve the branded demand Google converts so efficiently. The blended 4.5x is the number that reflects how the program actually works.
